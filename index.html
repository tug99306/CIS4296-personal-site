Projects

Temple CIS Co-op Approval System

The Co-op Approval System will be used by all CIS students who want to receive course credits for their internship opportunity. Our client, Dr. Eugene Kwatny is the primary professor of the professors in the Co-op system that students report back to during their internship. The main users of this system will be the CIS students looking to major in CS, IST or other math/computer driven degrees. This system will allow the students increased accessibility when trying to join the Co-op course and easier step by step documentation for getting credit for an internship.

Foodie Central Website

Foodie Central is meant to be a website for all of your recipe and cooking help needs. The aim of Foodie Central is to bring those who love food together. Through the site you can get professional advice on cooking and all things food related posted by professional chefs.

Clothing Store Database

A startup clothing company that is aiming to give back to the community that it was originally created in. As the company grows, their goal is to be able to give back to more and open more locations. We aim to increase our consumer diversity by offering clothes for all body types and all kinds of styles appealing to all genders. The database includes four different product groups with multiple items in each group. The database is curated to handle all aspects of operating an actual store, from products, to employees, and schedules.
